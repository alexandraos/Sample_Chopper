How to Use

  • Load: Upload an audio file or record system audio. The BPM is automatically detected.
  • Chop: Splits the audio into 16 beat-synced slices across the pads.
  • Play: Use your computer keyboard (1-4, Q-R, A-F, Z-V) or a MIDI controller.
  • Edit: Click a pad, then drag markers on the waveform to fine-tune its timing. Adjust Pitch & Speed in the editor.
  • Record: Capture your live performance and export it as an MP3!
