How to Use

  • Banks: Switch between Banks A-D to load different sounds.
  • Load & Chop: Upload an audio file. The BPM is automatically detected. It chops into 16 pads.
  • Edit: Click a pad, drag markers to adjust timing, or edit pitch/speed in the Sampler tab.
  • Sequence: Open the Arranger tab. Create 1-4 bar patterns. Hit REC PAT to record pads in real-time, perfectly quantized to the 16th note grid!
  • Song: Paint patterns onto the 32-bar timeline to build a full track.
  • Mix: Use the Mixer tab to balance levels and mute different banks.
  • Record: Capture your live pad drumming or full song playback directly to an MP3.
